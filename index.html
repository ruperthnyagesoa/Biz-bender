Applicants

Compliance with the principles of confidentiality

We send your resume to the client or check the recommendations at the request of the client only with your consent.

Long -term cooperation

We are discussing with you a specific position in the company of our client at the moment. Regardless of the result, we build long -term relationships with you.

Individual approach

Before you offer you a specific vacancy, we will definitely invite you for an interview, and discuss your professional experience, wishes and a specific vacancy, and we will also talk about current vacancies, give a council of interviews in the company.

Companies

Our principles

  • Analysis of the situation in the market and the level of compensation of packages in a particular market segment.
  • Studying the representation of specialists in together with the company developed criteria.
  • Interview with potential candidates, their professional and personal assessment, determination of motivation and compensation expectations. Obtaining consent to the transfer of information to the company from applicants.
  • Providing the company with a detailed interview that contains information about education, work experience, professional skills, additional knowledge.
  • The appointment of an interview with the selected candidates.
  • Collection and verification of recommendations for the final candidate (candidates).
  • Participation in the discussion with the candidate of the compensation package and the terms of the employment contract.
  • Monitoring the process of the candidate for work - documents, medical board, etc.
